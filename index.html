 Claudio Scordino

Computer Engineer, Ph.D.


Short bio

I am a Computer Engineer with PhD and 15-year experience on R&D projects about embedded real-time systems. My research activity is focused on improving real-time predictability of general-purpose, virtualized, mixed-criticality, service-oriented and/or multi-OS software architectures. I've been technical manager for several R&D projects funded by the European Commission, often in charge of Work Package leadership. My technical background covers the whole Linux ecosystem, including sporadic collaborations to the Linux kernel development (i.e. the SCHED_DEADLINE real-time scheduler). I currently work as Technical manager at the Huawei research center.
