  • HERCULES [H2020]: High-performance heterogeneous multi-core architectures

  • SECREDAS [ECSEL]: Product Security for Cross Domain Reliable Dependable Automated Systems

  • AMPERE [H2020]: A Model-driven development framework for highly Parallel and EneRgy-Efficient computation supporting multi-criteria optimization
